About

Rebel's Birds of Prey Centre started with a privately owned Northern Hawk Owl called Rebel. This plucky little bird ignited a passion in his owner which rapidly turned this hobby into the collection that the public can enjoy today.

Whether you are looking for a day out with the family, marvelling at a collection of 100 birds of prey, or seeking to experience life as a falconer, Rebel's has you covered. To learn more about the full range of services we offer, head on over to the visitor information page to find out more.
